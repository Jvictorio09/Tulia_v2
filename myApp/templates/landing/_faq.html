How long are the lessons?

Each lesson is 5-10 minutes, designed to fit into your busy schedule. You can complete multiple lessons per day or take your time—the choice is yours.

Do I need any special equipment?

Just your phone or computer with a microphone. That's it! All exercises work on any device with a modern browser.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes, you can cancel your Pro subscription at any time with no penalties. Your account will remain active until the end of your billing period, and you'll retain access to all Pro features until then.
